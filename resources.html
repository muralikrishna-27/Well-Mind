Immediate Coping Strategies

1. Wait 24 Hours

Give yourself one day. Use this time to reach out for help and remember that feelings change.

2. Reach Out to Someone

Call a friend, family member, or crisis hotline. You don't have to go through this alone.

3. Focus on Small Things

Think about small reasons to stay: a pet, an upcoming event, or simply seeing tomorrow's sunrise.

4. Create Distance

Remove yourself from harmful items and situations. Go to a safe place with other people.
